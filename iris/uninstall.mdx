---
title: "Uninstalling AnyNet IRIS"
description: "To uninstall AnyNet IRIS:"
---

<p>To uninstall AnyNet IRIS:</p>

<ol> <li>Using Windows <span>Control Panel</span>, select <span>Programs</span> &gt; <span>Uninstall a program</span>.</li> <li>Select AnyNet IRIS in the list.</li> <li>Select <span>Uninstall</span> to remove the application.</li> </ol>
