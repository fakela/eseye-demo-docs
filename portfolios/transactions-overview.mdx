<h3>Single purchase</h3>

<p>A single purchase consists of a one-off fee that is added to the transaction queue for approval and subsequent display on the customer's invoice. This fee could be either <a href="/packages/manage-contract-information#Terminat2" target="_blank">Termination Price</a>, consultancy charges, VPN charges, hardware sales items or set-up charges, but it does not include usage consumption SIM based charges. </p>

<p>These charges are processed by the MNO through the Invoicing entity. This entity is usually the service provider and is legally and financially recognised as the party entitled to receive payment.It is the business responsible for raising the invoice. For example, an MNO using the solution to incorporate charges into the monthly invoice.</p>

<h6>Single purchase journey map</h6>

<ol> <li>MNO: Delivers services and monitors usage.</li> <li>Billing system: Calculates charges and creates invoices.</li> <li>Invoicing entity: A financially recognized organization (such as Eseye, TELUS, MTN, etc.) responsible for creating invoices for its customers. The invoicing entity generates invoices for active transactions.</li> <li>Customer payment: Facilitated through multiple payment methods and handled by the invoicing entity.</li> </ol>

<h3>Subscriptions</h3>

<p>A subscription is a recurring fee that is translated into a portfolio and aggregated into a single transaction. This fee could be either VPN charges, <a href="/packages/manage-contract-information#SIM" target="_blank">SIM Price</a>, <a href="/packages/manage-contract-information#Contract2" target="_blank">Contract Escape Price</a>, <a href="/packages/manage-contract-information#Unused" target="_blank">Unused Rebate Price</a>, <a href="/packages/manage-contract-information#Activati" target="_blank">Activation Price</a>, <a href="/packages/manage-contract-information#Terminat2" target="_blank">Termination Price</a> etc.</p>

<p>For example, a transaction consists of multiple subscriptions billed quarterly, implying that 4 subscriptions combines into an annual transaction. A created subscription is added to the transaction queue after approval and, once processed by the billing system, it is deleted.</p>

<h6>Subscription journey map</h6>

<ol> <li>Approving created subscriptions.</li> <li>Adding approved subscriptions to the transaction queue.</li> <li>Processing queued subscriptions.</li> <li>Deleting post-processed subscriptions.</li> </ol>
