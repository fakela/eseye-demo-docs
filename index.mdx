## Frequently asked questions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Connectivity">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="What is multi-IMSI and why does it matter?">
        Multi-IMSI lets a single SIM hold multiple network identities, so devices can switch between operators for better coverage and resilience. See [Understanding multi-IMSI functionality](/getting-started/multi-imsi).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How does roaming and localisation work?">
        Eseye combines roaming with localisation to keep devices connected to the strongest local network while avoiding permanent-roaming restrictions. Read more in [Roaming](/getting-started/roaming) and [Localisation](/getting-started/localisation).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="What APN should my devices use?">
        Your APN determines how device traffic is routed to your cloud or network. See [Access Point Names (APNs)](/connectivity/access-point-name) for setup details.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How does Eseye route traffic to my cloud?">
        Traffic flows from the device through Eseye's points of presence (PoPs) to your destination. Start with the [Connectivity overview](/connectivity/connectivity-intro).
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="SIMs & eSIM">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="What is eSIM and how is it different from a physical SIM?">
        eSIM (eUICC) lets you provision and switch operator profiles remotely, without swapping hardware. Learn the basics in [About eSIM technology](/getting-started/esim/esim).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I activate my SIMs?">
        Activation is managed from your portal alongside the rest of your estate. See [Managing SIMs](/managing-my-estate/managing-sims).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="My SIM isn't connecting — what should I check?">
        Work through the common causes and fixes in [SIM not working](/troubleshooting/sim-not-working).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="What do I do about a lost or stolen SIM?">
        Suspend the affected SIM to protect your estate and follow the steps in [Stolen SIMs](/troubleshooting/stolen-sims).
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Billing & estate">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="How does billing and invoicing work?">
        Understand charges, invoices, and reporting in the [Billing and invoices](/billing/billing-intro) guide.
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="What is the Infinity portal?">
        Infinity is Eseye's platform for managing connectivity, SIMs, and devices across your estate. See the [Infinity introduction](/infinity/introduction).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I manage my estate day to day?">
        From activating SIMs to running reports and controlling invoicing — start with [Managing my estate](/managing-my-estate/managing-my-estate).
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="APIs & developers">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="How do I get started as a developer?">
        Follow the developer onboarding in [Getting started for developers](/developer-docs/getting-started-for-developers).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="How do I authenticate and start using the APIs?">
        Learn how to access and call the REST APIs in [Start using APIs](/developer-docs/start-using-apis).
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Which API manages the SIM lifecycle?">
        The SIM API covers activation, status, and lifecycle operations. See the [SIM API introduction](/api/sim/simapi-intro).
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
