title: "Troubleshooting SIM cards"
description: "Complete the following steps to eliminate common problems:"
---

<p>Complete the following steps to eliminate common problems:</p>

<ol> <li>Ensure that the device is powered on.</li> <li> Ensure that the SIM card is inserted correctly, making sure that the gold plate is in contact with the reader.  <br />For help with the Hera 604v5 router, see: <a href="/hardware-products/hera/600-series/getting-started604v5">Installing the Hera 604v5</a>. </li> <li>Ensure that antennas are attached to the device.</li> <li>Ensure that there is network coverage in the area where the device exists.</li> <li>Leave on for 20 minutes, turn off for 10 seconds, and then power on the device.</li> </ol>

<Tip>If these steps fail to help, then contact Support: <a href="mailto:support@eseye.com">support@eseye.com</a>.</Tip>
