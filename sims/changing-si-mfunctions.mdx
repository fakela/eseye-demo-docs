---
title: "Changing SIM functions"
description: "You can request Support to alter the functions/services (SMS, data, etc) of a SIM."
---

<p>You can request Support to alter the functions/services (SMS, data, etc) of a SIM.</p>

<Note>Eseye may charge a standard rate for functions that are not agreed prior to use.</Note>

<p>Use a pre-formatted <a href="mailto:support@eseye.com?subject=Altering%20the%20functions%20available%20on%20my%20SIM%20card(s)&Body=Hello%20Support,%0D%0DMy%20SIAM%20account%20username%20is:%0DPlease%20change%20the%20function%20of%20the%20following%20ICCIDs%20(no%20quotation%20marks):%0D%0D%0D%0D%0DCurrent%20available%20functions:%0D%0DChange(s)%20I%20would%20like%20to%20make%20(add%20or%20remove%20function):%0D%0DI%20understand%20that%20I%20will%20be%20charged%20at%20a%20standard%20rate%20if%20I%20add%20functions%20that%20are%20not%20detailed%20in%20the%20SIM%20contract.%0DPlease%20reply%20to%20this%20email%20to%20notify%20me%20when%20the%20SIMs%20are%20changed.%0D%0DKind%20regards,%0D%0D">email</a> to request a change of function.</p>

<p>Use the link provided, or create your own email to support@eseye.com as follows:</p>

<ul> <li><span>Subject:</span> Altering the functions available on my SIM(s)</li> <li>You must include all of the following body text: <ul><li>Infinity Classic account username.</li><li>List the SIM ICCIDs whose function you want to change (without quotation marks). </li><li>List the functions you want to enable or disable.</li><li>I understand that I will be charged at a standard rate if I add functions that are not detailed in the SIM contract.</li></ul></li> </ul>
