<p>You can send emails using AnyNet SIM cards.</p>

<h2 id="Configurationsettings">Configuration settings</h2>

<table> <col /> <col /> <tbody> <tr> <td> <span>SMTP Relay:</span>  </td> <td> 192.168.111.3</td> </tr> <tr> <td> <span>Port:</span>  </td> <td>25</td> </tr> <tr> <td> <span>Recommended From email address domain:</span>  </td> <td> eseye.net </td> </tr> </tbody> </table>

<Tip>The external IP address for the mail relay resolves to <span>eseye.net</span>. We recommend using the eseye.net domain as the <span>From</span> email address in the device to allow successful negotiation of some email SPAM filters.</Tip>
