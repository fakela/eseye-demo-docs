<p> International Mobile Subscriber Identity number. Identifies a cellular network subscriber. The network operator that provided the IMSI uses it to authenticate a SIM (and the IoT device containing the SIM) on the network. </p>

<Note>Contains between 15 and 16 numeric digits.</Note>
