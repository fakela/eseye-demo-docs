<p>Coordinated Universal Time. A universal standard for time (defined in <a href="https://www.itu.int/rec/R-REC-TF.460/en" target="_blank">ITU-R TF.460-6</a>) that regulates clocks. UTC time is mapped to geographical timezones but does not change for regional adjustments such as daylight savings.</p>
