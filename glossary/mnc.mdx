<p>Mobile Network Code. A two or three digit unique identifier for a home network within a country, which is identified by the MCC. The MNC is the second part of the IMSI, after the MCC. For more information, see <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mobile_country_code" target="_blank">Mobile network codes listed by country</a>.</p>
