<p>A contract that enables one operator to provide service to subscribers (IMSIs) that do not belong to their network. The services available to roaming devices may differ from the services provided by the home network operator.</p>

<Note>Roaming agreements are not permanent and may change over time, which can impact subscriber connectivity.</Note>
