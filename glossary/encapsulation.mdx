<p>In networking, the process of wrapping data packets within additional headers and trailers as they traverse different layers of the network protocol stack. Each protocol layer wrapper contributes relevant information for proper transmission and delivery of the original data payload, enabling interoperability across different network layers and technologies. The data receiver then extracts the original payload from the wrappers.</p>

<p>Encapsulation provides various functionalities and services required for reliable and efficient data transmission, including addressing, routing, error detection, flow control, security, and more. </p>
