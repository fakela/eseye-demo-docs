---
title: "Radio Access Technology (RAT)"
description: "The underlying physical connection method that a device uses to connect to a cellular network, such as Bluetooth, WiFi, GSM, UMTS, LTE or 5G NR. A device may support one or more RATs."
---

<p>The underlying physical connection method that a device uses to connect to a cellular network, such as Bluetooth, WiFi, GSM, UMTS, LTE or 5G NR. A device may support one or more RATs. </p>
