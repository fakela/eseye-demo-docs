---
title: "System lifecycle management"
---

<p> </p>

<p>System lifecycle management is the process of managing the entire lifecycle of software and firmware elements that runs on IoT devices. It is critical for ensuring that devices remain secure, reliable, and up-to-date throughout their lifecycle, which is essential for providing a positive user experience and protecting against security threats.</p>

<p>The lifecycle management process involves:</p>

<ol> <li>Defining and managing software and firmware requirements for devices.</li> <li>Developing and testing software and firmware on devices.</li> <li>Deploying software and firmware to devices in a secure and controlled manner.</li> <li>Monitoring and managing the performance of software and firmware on devices.</li> <li>Providing ongoing maintenance and support for software and firmware on devices, including managing updates, security patches, bug fixes, and so on.</li> <li>Retiring software, firmware, and devices when they reach the end of their useful life.</li> </ol>

<p>We supply AnyNet SMARTconnect™ to help you deploy and maintain your device software and firmware. For more information, see <a href="/software-products/anynet-smartconnect-intro">About AnyNet SMARTconnect™</a>.</p>

<p>Lifecycle management is becoming increasingly important as the number of connected devices continues to grow, and the complexity of software and firmware running on these devices increases. </p>
