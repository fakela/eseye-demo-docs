<h2>Establishing a cellular connection on the Intelligent Cloud Connect</h2>

<p>Ensure you have connected the terminal antenna to the <span>Antenna</span> connection. <span></span></p>

<p>To power on and establish your cellular connection:</p>

<ol> <li> Fully insert the SIM card into the Intelligent Cloud Connect.  <span><strong>Tip: </strong> You can see how to insert the SIM on the underside of the terminal. The socket is a push-to-insert, push-to-release type socket. When the SIM is properly inserted, it clicks into place and sits flush with the edge of the SIM slot. </span> <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/gemaltoboxconnections.jpg" alt="Gemaltoboxconnections" />  </li> <li>  Turn on the Intelligent Cloud Connect.   <br />The Intelligent Cloud Connect connects to a cellular network using the Cinterion® PLS62‑W Wireless Module, and is now ready to receive security and identity information from AWS. </li> </ol>
