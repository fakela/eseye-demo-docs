---
title: "CCID – request unique SIM number (ICCID)"
description: "This command returns the unique Integrated Circuit Card Identifier (ICCID) of the installed SIM card."
---

<p>This command returns the unique Integrated Circuit Card Identifier (ICCID) of the installed SIM card.</p>

<table> <col /> <col /> <col /> <thead> <tr> <th>Type</th> <th>Syntax</th> <th>Returned Result</th> </tr> </thead> <tbody> <tr> <td>Test</td> <td> AT+CCID=? </td> <td> OK </td> </tr> <tr> <td>Execute</td> <td> AT+CCID </td> <td> +CCID: &lt;SIMnumber&gt; <br />  <br />OK <br />where <span>&lt;SIMnumber&gt;</span> is the unique ICCID for the attached SIM. <br />or <br /><span>ERROR</span> – the command failed. </td> </tr> </tbody> </table>

<h4>Example</h4>

<p>AT+CCID</p>

<p>+CCID: 8944531233020460000</p>

<p> </p>

<p>OK</p>
