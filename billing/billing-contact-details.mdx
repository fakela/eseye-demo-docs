<p>If you have not received an invoice, or you cannot process a payment, check that your contact details are correct in Infinity Classic.</p>

<p>To check your contact details:</p>

<ol> <li>Log in to Infinity Classic: <a href="https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login">https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login</a></li> <li>Select <span>Finance</span> &gt; <span>Details</span> to view the <span>Finance details</span> page.</li> <li> If you need to edit the details, select <span>Edit</span>. <br />The Update Finance Details page appears. </li> <li> Make your changes, then select <span>Save</span>. </li> </ol>

<p>If your contact details are correct and you are having problems processing a payment or receiving invoices, contact Accounts: <a href="mailto:accounts@eseye.com">accounts@eseye.com</a></p>
