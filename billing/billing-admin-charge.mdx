<p>We recommend you adhere to IoT best practice to ensure that your devices connect efficiently to a network. For more information, see <a href="/connectivity/device-configuration#Configur" target="_blank">Configuring devices for connection efficiency</a>.</p>

<p>Devices that attempt network connection more than 20 times a day are not adhering to best practice. Eseye are charged an AWS billing function when a customer device exceeds more than 100 connection attempts a day. At this point, we will invoice you an administration charge to cover our resulting costs.</p>
