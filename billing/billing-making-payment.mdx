<p>You can pay Eseye using the following methods through the Infinity Classic portal:</p>

<ul> <li>Direct Debit – for UK customers with bills under £2000</li> <li>Bank to bank transfer (BACS payment)</li> <li> Debit or credit card, with the option for repeat monthly billing</li> <li>PayPal</li> </ul>

<Accordion title="Setting up a Direct Debit payment">
 <p>Only for UK customers with bills under £2000. Notice is given ten days in advance. Payment is usually collected a month in arrears.</p> <p>To set up a direct debit:</p> <ol> <li>Log in to Infinity Classic: <a href="https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login">https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login</a></li> <li>Select <span>Finance</span> &gt; <span>Overview</span> to view the <span>Finance overview</span> page.</li> <li> Under <span>Finance settings</span>, alongside <span>Payment method</span>, select <img src="/images/resources/images/billing/edit_ic.png" alt="Edit Ic" />. <br />The Payment Settings Change Request dialog box appears. </li> <li> Select the <span>Direct Debit(Monthly)</span> option. </li> <li> Select <span>Request change</span>. <br />The Direct Debit Setup Request form appears. </li> <li>Select <img src="/images/resources/images/billing/requestddform_ic.png" alt="Requestddform Ic" /> to download the <span>DDI MANDATE NEW.doc</span> form.</li> <li>Complete the form details, then save the form.</li> <li>Select <span>Choose file</span> to browse and select the saved form.</li> <li>Select <span>Open</span> to attach the form to the Direct Debit Setup Request.</li> <li> Select <span>Upload Form</span> to send the form to Eseye. <br />If the upload is successful, a Direct Debit Request Sent Successfully message appears in the Payment Settings Change Request window. <br />If the upload is not successful, contact Support: <a href="mailto:support@eseye.com?subject=SIAM: Could not upload Direct Debit">support@eseye.com</a>. </li> <li>Eseye will send you an email confirming that the direct debit is set up.</li> </ol> 
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Making a BACS payment">
 <ol> <li>Log in to Infinity Classic: <a href="https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login">https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login</a></li> <li> View a PDF invoice to find the Eseye BACS Payment Details. <br />For more information, see<a href="/billing/billing-locate-invoice">Viewing an invoice</a>. </li> <li> Set up the payment using your bank account. </li> <li> Notify Eseye that you have paid the bill using a BACS payment: <a href="mailto:accounts@eseye.com?subject=BACS Payment: <Reference> <AmountPaid>">accounts@eseye.com</a>.  <span><strong>Note: </strong>Include the following information in your email: invoice number as a payment reference, as well as the amount paid.</span> </li> </ol> 
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Paying with Paypal or a debit or credit card">
 <p>Eseye accepts payments from:</p> <ul> <li> VISA Credit, VISA Debit, VISA Electron</li> <li>Debit Mastercard, Mastercard</li> <li>Paypal</li> </ul> <p>After you complete an initial credit or debit card payment, Eseye offers a recurring payment service.</p> <div> <h3>Before you begin</h3> <p>You will need:</p> <ul> <li>Purchase Order number</li> <li>Debit or Credit card, unless you are using Paypal</li> </ul> </div> <p>To pay the outstanding bill:</p> <ol> <li>Log in to Infinity Classic: <a href="https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login">https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login</a></li> <li> Select <span>Finance</span> &gt; <span>Overview</span> to view the <span>Finance overview</span> page. </li> <li> If you want to pay an amount, then select <span>Make payment by Credit Card</span> alongside either of the following amounts: <br /><span>Amount due</span> – overdue amount. You owe this immediately. <br /><span>Account balance</span> – total amount owed, including the overdue amount. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/billing/payoutstandingbill.png" alt="Payoutstandingbill" />  <br />The shopping cart appears, with the amount listed. </li> <li> Alternatively, if you want to pay a specific amount, enter the required amount in the <span>Amount</span> field, then select <span>Make Ad-hoc Payment By Credit Card</span>. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/billing/payoutstandingbillspecific.png" alt="Payoutstandingbillspecific" />  <br />The shopping cart appears, with the specified amount listed. </li> <li>Select <span>Checkout</span>.</li> <li>On the <span>Billing Address</span> tab, ensure the Billing Address is correct.</li> <li>If you need to change the billing address details, select <span>Edit this address</span>.</li> <li>Select <span>Next Step</span> to view the <span>Options</span> tab.</li> <li>Type a mandatory <span>Order Summary</span> to help you identify this order.</li> <li>Type a mandatory <span>PO Number</span> to reference your Purchase Order.</li> <li>Type <span>Additional Notes</span>.</li> <li>Select <span>Next Step</span> to view the <span>Review Cart</span> tab.</li> <li> Review the payment, then select <span>Make Payment</span>. <br />The Sage Pay - Payment Selection window appears. </li> <li>Select Paypal or your payment card provider.</li> <li> If required, enter your debit or credit card details. <br />You may be required to authenticate your card. </li> <li>Complete the payment.</li> <li> If the payment fails, check that your Infinity Classic contact details are correct. <br />For more information, see <a href="/billing/billing-contact-details">Editing your Infinity Classic contact details</a>. </li> </ol> 
</Accordion>

<p>If you have any problems, contact Accounts: <a href="mailto:accounts@eseye.com">accounts@eseye.com</a></p>
