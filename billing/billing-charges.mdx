<h2 id="Aboutactivationcharges">About activation charges</h2>

<p>Eseye may apply a charge when a SIM is first activated.</p>

<p>This charge may cover the cost of the SIM, or provide an initial testing period. Speak to your Account Manager for more information.</p>

<h2 id="Aboutservicecharges">About service charges</h2>

<p>Customers are billed for their out-of-bundle SIM usage every month, as well as a service charge per active SIM. </p>

<p>Service charges cover the following: </p>

<ul> <li> Active subscription on the mobile network <span><strong>Note: </strong>Suspended SIMs are active SIMs.</span> </li> <li> Any bundled usage</li> </ul>

<h2 id="Aboutpostpaymonthlybillingcharges">About post pay monthly billing charges</h2>

<p>Customers using post pay billing are billed in arrears for out-of-bundle SIM usage and service charges for each SIM with an <span>active</span> status at the end of a month.</p>

<p>A newly activated SIM incurs a partial service charge from the day the SIM is activated for the number of days the SIM is active. </p>

<p><img src="/images/resources/images/billing/postpaybilling.png" alt="Postpaybilling" /> </p>

<p>If you terminate a SIM part way through the month, the SIM incurs a partial service charge for the number of days it was active during that month, and is billed in arrears on the following invoice. For more information, see <a href="#SIM">About SIM termination fees</a>.</p>

<h2 id="Aboutprepaymonthlybillingcharges">About prepay monthly billing charges</h2>

<p>For customers using the historic prepay billing system, each SIM with an <span>active</span> status on the account is billed a service charge a month in advance. A newly activated SIM incurs a partial service charge from the day the SIM is activated for the number of days the SIM is active. This is billed in arrears on the first invoice, in addition to the monthly service charge in advance for the following month. Out-of-bundle SIM usage charges are charged in arrears.</p>

<p><img src="/images/resources/images/billing/prepaybilling.png" alt="Prepaybilling" /> </p>

<p>If you terminate a SIM part way through a month, there is no refund for the service charge that you have already paid for that SIM. For more information, see <a href="#SIM">About SIM termination fees</a>.</p>

<h2 id="Aboutprepayannualbillingservicecharges">About prepay annual billing service charges</h2>

<p>You pay service charges as a lump sum annually when you activate a SIM on an annual Service Contract. Service renewal then occurs on the anniversary of activation for each SIM. </p>

<p>Customers who pay annual service charges for their SIMs will receive monthly bills, which include:</p>

<ul> <li>The amount of out-of-bundle services used in the last month</li> <li>The annual service charge for each SIM that was activated during the past month</li> <li>Advance service charge renewal for each SIM approaching its activation anniversary within the next month</li> </ul>

<p>For example:</p>

<p>A SIM activated on 10/03/2021 is billed for service charges from the 10/03/2021 through to the 09/03/2022 on the 01/04/2021 invoice. The 01/05/2021 invoice includes the SIM's usage for the month of April, but no service charges as these are already billed. The following year, the annual service charge renewal for that SIM will appear on the 01/03/2022 bill, and the renewed service will start on the 10/03/2022.</p>

<p><img src="/images/resources/images/billing/annualbilling.png" alt="Annualbilling" /> </p>

<h2 id="Adjustinginvoicesforportfolioorpackageamendmentandpackagemigration">Adjusting invoices for portfolio or package amendment, and package migration</h2>

<p>Customers can request Sales Admin to amend their portfolio or package (such as adding a new IMSI, adding a new zone, or changing the pricing) or migrate their SIMs to a different package.</p>

<p>Any changes made within a month, and their subsequent related charges, are backdated to midnight of the first of that month. For example, if you make a portfolio or package change on 10th August 2023 at 13h56, the invoice will display the change as occurring on 1st August 2023 at 00h00.</p>

<h3>Package item amendment requests cut off</h3>

<p>Package item amendment requests are processed until UK close of business on the 24th of each month, and will appear on the next invoice. For invoice processing purposes, all amendment requests that occur from the 25th of the month are not processed until the first working day of the following month.</p>

<h3>Package migration requests cut off</h3>

<p>Requests to migrate SIMs from one package to another are processed up until three days before the end of the month, and will appear on the next invoice. For invoice processing purposes, all package migration requests occurring within these three days are not processed until the first working day of the following month.</p>

<h2 id="AboutSIMterminationfees">About SIM termination fees</h2>

<p>You can terminate a SIM at any time. If you terminate a SIM that has a monthly or annual service charge, the following charges apply:</p>

<ul> <li> If a SIM is still in contract, you are liable for the service charge to the end of that contract. You will receive a separate termination invoice with the remaining service charges in one lump sum, unless you have made a prior alternative arrangement with Accounts. </li> </ul>

<ul> <li>  If a SIM is no longer in contract, you may terminate it without incurring further service charges. The deactivated SIM may appear on the next bill to allow for any usage within the last month, prior to termination. SMS, data and voice overages are billed in arrears.  </li> </ul>

<p> Service charges are non-refundable. </p>

<Note>Termination is not instant – Eseye must first verify the termination.</Note>

<Note>Terminating a SIM is not the same as suspending a SIM. A suspended SIM will continue connecting to the network, and service charges will continue. However, you cannot use the SIM card in its suspended state. A terminated SIM cannot connect to the network. </Note>
