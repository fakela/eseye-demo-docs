<p>Eseye invoices are sent via email. If you have not received an invoice, ensure your <span>Billing Contact Details</span> are correct in Infinity Classic.</p>

<p>For more information, see <a href="/billing/billing-contact-details">Editing your Infinity Classic contact details</a>.</p>

<p>To view an invoice:</p>

<ol> <li>Log in to Infinity Classic: <a href="https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login">https://siam3.eseye.com/user/login</a></li> <li> Select <span>Finance</span> &gt; <span>Transactions</span>. <br />Account transactions are listed in chronological order, with the most recent transaction first. </li> <li> Expand the number of results returned to 100 per page. <br /> <img src="/images/resources/images/billing/expandresultsperpage.png" alt="Expandresultsperpage" />  </li> <li>In the <span>Type</span> column, locate the relevant <span>Invoice</span>.</li> <li> Alongside the <span>Invoice</span>, in the <span>Actions</span> column, select any of the following icons to view the file: <table> <col /> <col /> <tbody> <tr> <td> <img src="/images/resources/images/billing/pdf_ic.png" alt="Pdf Ic" /> </td> <td>Download the invoice for viewing in your default PDF application.</td> </tr> <tr> <td> <img src="/images/resources/images/billing/print_ic.png" alt="Print Ic" /> </td> <td>Open the invoice in your browser window for printing without downloading it.</td> </tr> <tr> <td> <img src="/images/resources/images/billing/csv_ic.png" alt="Csv Ic" /> </td> <td>Download a detailed CSV file, containing a cost breakdown.</td> </tr> <tr> <td> <img src="/images/resources/images/billing/xml_ic.png" alt="Xml Ic" /> </td> <td>Download an XML invoice.</td> </tr> <tr> <td> <img src="/images/resources/images/billing/email_ic.png" alt="Email Ic" /> </td> <td>Immediately email the invoice to yourself as an attached PDF.</td> </tr> </tbody> </table> </li> </ol>

<Tip>For examples of the PDF and CSV invoices, see <a href="https://docs.eseye.com/Content/Resources/Files/PDFInvoiceExample.pdf" target="_blank">PDF Invoice Example.pdf</a> or <a href="https://docs.eseye.com/Content/Resources/Files/CSVnvoiceExample.pdf" target="_blank">CSV Invoice Example.pdf</a>.</Tip>
