<p>Eseye differentiates between SMS, data, and voice billing:</p>

<ul> <li>SMS – the number of SMS messages sent by a SIM</li> <li>Data – the mobile data used by a SIM</li> <li> Voice – the voice calls used by a SIM <span><strong>Note: </strong>Voice is no longer offered, as it is not required for M2M communication. Voice is included in the billing topics for legacy reasons.</span> </li> </ul>

<p>Usage is calculated within a calendar month, and billed in arrears.</p>

<p>For simplicity, SMS, data and voice charges are billed in priority zones, designed to provide standardised pricing across multinational regions. The zones are defined in your Service Contract and tailored for your use. Zone 1 often includes your countries of highest deployment. Where device deployment is required globally, further zones are tailored to group countries and networks with similar cost tariffs.</p>

<p>You can include the usage type in a bundle that you allocate to your SIM cards.</p>

<h2 id="UnderstandingdatausageperSIM">Understanding data usage per SIM</h2>

<p>Use the CSV Invoice to understand how much data each SIM uses.</p>

<p>To view SIM data usage in the CSV Invoice:</p>

<ol> <li> Select the row containing the <span>ItemRef</span><span>Data</span> (Column A). </li> <li> Navigate to <span>Description</span> (column H) to view a description of Data. </li> <li> Total data usage in bytes = <span>Quantity</span> + <span>InBundleQuantity</span>, where: <br /><span>Quantity</span> (column K) is the number of excess (overage) bytes used <br /><span>InBundleQuantity</span> (column S) is the number of bytes used inside the bundled amount </li> <li> For kilobytes, divide the total by 1024. </li> <li> For megabytes, divide the total by 1048576. </li> </ol>
