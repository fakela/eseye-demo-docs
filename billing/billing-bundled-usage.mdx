<p>You can prepay monthly for SIM usage bundles, which will give you a better price for data or SMS usage than pay-as-you-go. Bundles are included in a SIM's monthly service charge. For example, a SIM may have a 250MB data bundle, where it can use up to 250MB of data a month before incurring further charges.</p>

<Note>Any usage amount in excess of the bundle is known as the overage. </Note>

<Note>Voice is no longer offered, as it is not required for M2M communication. Voice is included in the billing topics for legacy reasons.</Note>

<p>On the CSV invoice, the following columns show the SIM usage:</p>

<ul> <li> <span>Quantity</span> – bundle overage </li> <li><span>Amount</span> – the overage cost</li> <li><span>BundleQuantity</span> – total bundle allowance</li> <li> <span>InBundleQuantity</span> – usage amount within the bundle allowance</li> </ul>

<p>Late billed usage from a network operator will cause a bundle adjustment for the previous month. This is billed in arrears.</p>
