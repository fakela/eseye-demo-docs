<h2 id="January2024">January 2024</h2>

<h3>Limited access to O2 UK Cat-M1 networks</h3>

<p>At Eseye, our multi-IMSI SIMs connect on the most appropriate network in any given situation. We work with network partners to ensure that you achieve nearly 100% connectivity for your devices.</p>

<p>Our network partner O2 has informed us that they are restricting their UK Cat-M1 network services until Q3, 2024. This will allow O2 some time to consider the implementation of an approvals process for devices roaming on their network and work with partners to find a resolution.</p>

<p>We are seeking clarity on any certification process that O2 may introduce, and the timelines for the removal of restrictions. In the meantime, we advise you to work closely with us if you have plans to deploy LPWAN solutions in the UK and globally, so that you can leverage the full range of connectivity options available for your device.</p>

<p>We also suggest using our <a href="https://www.eseye.com/services/iot-device-testing-validation/" target="_blank">Device Validation</a> service to minimize the risk of your device failing any future MNO approvals process. Our Device Validation service will also ensure your device is robust and resilient to network changes.</p>
