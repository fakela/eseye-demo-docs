<p>Activates single or multiple SIMs. Eseye SIMs are available, ready to become active. Before you make one or more SIMs active, you must have a signed Service Contract form agreeing to the associated costs. You must associate the SIMs with a package, using the tariffID.</p>

<p>Use activateSIMs to reprovision terminated SIMs. You must agree to contract terms before the SIM is reprovisioned.</p>

<Tip>We recommend that you only activate up to 300 SIM cards per POST, as internet latency issues may introduce errors.</Tip>
