<p>Suspends multiple SIMs. The current SIM state must allow suspension – you cannot suspend a SIM that is already suspended, pending termination, or terminated. </p>

<p>Suspension starts immediately, but the SIM will initially deprovision. The suspension process takes up to two hours until the status displays as suspended. You cannot use the SIM card in its suspended state. </p>

<Note>When suspended, the SIM will continue to connect to the network, and service charges will continue. However, usage charges will not continue. For more information, see Understanding service charges.</Note>

<p>If required, you can reverse the suspension and reactivate the SIM card. For more information, see <a href="/api/tigrillo/unsuspend-sims">POST /Japi/Tigrillo/unsuspendSIMs</a>.</p>
