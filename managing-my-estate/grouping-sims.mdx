<p>SIM Groups and Collections enable users to manage or view multiple SIMs, but each serves a distinct purpose within the Infinity portal.</p>

<ul> <li>Use SIM Groups to tag SIMs, allowing you to categorise or identify them easily. These tags can also appear on your invoice, helping with billing categorization. For more information, see <a href="/managing-my-estate/manage-tagging-sims">About SIM Groups</a>.</li> <li>Use Collections to organise SIMs based on specific criteria or attributes. For more information, see <a href="/managing-my-estate/manage-sim-collections">About SIM Collections</a>.</li> </ul>
