<p>Hera routers enable communication between IoT devices and networks, efficiently directing data traffic and implementing security measures to protect the local network and devices from unauthorised access and various cyber threats.</p>

<p><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/herarouterfamily.png" alt="Herarouterfamily" /> </p>

<p>The Hera routers are designed to deliver life-long zero-touch service, with highly resilient data connectivity delivered within M2M grade hardware in a robust aluminium casing. This makes the Hera series an ideal high-speed 3G/4G network failover solution.</p>

<p>Hera routers are preconfigured to the customer's unique specifications for easy deployment. </p>

<p>Coupled with an AnyNet SIM, Hera routers can connect globally across 190 countries via 700 mobile networks, ensuring near 100% connectivity. Hera routers have optional WiFi capability and fallback for post installation management and reconfiguration. Depending on the model, the Hera is fitted with one or four Ethernet ports and the ability to support one or more WAN ports.</p>

<h2 id="Intelligentdevicemonitoring">Intelligent device monitoring</h2>

<p>Using the Hera together with Eseye's Infinity IoT Connectivity Management Platform, your IoT device data is transparently accessible, enabling intelligent condition monitoring and alert notifications. Infinity offers comprehensive connectivity management and SIM performance assurance. For more information, see <a href="/infinity/introduction">About Infinity</a>.</p>
