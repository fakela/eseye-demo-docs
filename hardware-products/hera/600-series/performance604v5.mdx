---
title: "Viewing Hera data performance"
description: "Model: Hera 604 Version: v5"
---

<div> <p><span>Model:</span> Hera 604 <span>Version:</span> v5</p> </div>

<p>You can view the daily performance of the Hera connection, including Network operator and signal strength, and data transmitted and received.</p>

<p>To view data performance:</p>

<ol> <li> Using the Hera user interface, navigate to <span>Diagnostics &amp; Maintenance</span> &gt; <span>Data Logging</span> &gt; <span>Performance</span>. <br />The Performance page appears, displaying data for the current date. </li> <li> In the <span>Network operator and signal strength</span> graph, mouseover the bar above the graph to view the network operator name. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/performancenetworkoperator.png" alt="Performancenetworkoperator" />  </li> <li> Mouseover the data points on the graph to see the recorded signal strength value, date and time stamped. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/performancesignalstrength.png" alt="Performancesignalstrength" />  </li> <li> In the <span>Data transmitted and received</span> graph, mouseover the bars to view the amount of data sent (displayed in orange) or received (displayed in green). <br />The amounts are displayed in kilobytes. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/performancedatatransmitted.png" alt="Performancedatatransmitted" />  </li> <li> Beneath either graph, drag the slider right to zoom in on the time scale, or left to zoom out. Both graphs will zoom in or out. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/performancezoomslider.png" alt="Performancezoomslider" />  </li> </ol>

<p>To change the viewing date:</p>

<ol> <li> Alongside the date, select <span>&lt;</span> (previous day) or <span>&gt;</span> (next day) to change dates. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/performancedate.png" alt="Performancedate" />  </li> <li> Alternatively, select the date, then select either the day, month or year, and drag to the left or right.  <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/performancedateselect.png" alt="Performancedateselect" />  <br />Select <img src="/images/resources/images/hera/tick_ic.png" alt="Tick Ic" /> to view the selected date, or <img src="/images/resources/images/hera/cancel_ic.png" alt="Cancel Ic" /> to cancel the selection. </li> </ol>

<p>To change the viewing time:</p>

<ol> <li> Drag and drop the left slider to the required start time. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/starttime.png" alt="Starttime" />  </li> <li> Drag and drop the right slider to the required end time. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/endtime.png" alt="Endtime" />  </li> <li> Drag and drop the span between the sliders to maintain the viewing period but shift the start and end times. <br /><img src="/images/resources/images/hera/maintainperiod.png" alt="Maintainperiod" />  </li> </ol>
