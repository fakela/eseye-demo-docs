<h2 id="Bottompanelfunctions">Bottom panel functions</h2>

<h4>Link indicators</h4>

<table> <col /> <col /> <thead> <tr> <th>LED indication</th> <th>Description</th> </tr> </thead> <tbody> <tr> <td>No LEDs</td> <td>No connection.</td> </tr> <tr> <td>Solid yellow, solid green</td> <td>The Ethernet cable is attached between the Hera and the Ethernet client device. The port is available and can transfer data up to 100 Mbps.</td> </tr> <tr> <td> Solid yellow, <br />green off </td> <td>The Ethernet cable is attached between the Hera and the Ethernet client device. The port is available and can transfer data up to 1000 Mbps.</td> </tr> <tr> <td> Flashing yellow, green off </td> <td>Data transfer in progress (up to 1000 Mbps).</td> </tr> <tr> <td>Flashing yellow and green</td> <td>Data transfer in progress (up to 100 Mbps).</td> </tr> </tbody> </table>

<h4>Available ports</h4>

<p>The Hera 604 v6 has four integrated 10/100/1000baseT/TX Ethernet ports for WAN or LAN. You can use one or more ports as the Ethernet LAN. </p>

<Tip>Should you require additional LAN or WAN configurations, contact Support.</Tip>
