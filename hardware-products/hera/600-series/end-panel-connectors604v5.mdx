<h4>Available ports</h4>

<p>The Hera 604 v5 has four integrated 10/100baseT/TX Ethernet ports for WAN or LAN. You can use one or more ports as the Ethernet LAN. </p>

<Tip>While you can set up any <span>Link</span> port as the Ethernet WAN port, we recommend you use <span>Link 4</span> for this purpose. For more information, see<a href="/hardware-products/hera/600-series/basic-settings604v5#Configur" target="_blank">Configuring the Ethernet WAN</a>.</Tip>

<Tip>Should you require additional LAN or WAN configurations, contact Support.</Tip>
